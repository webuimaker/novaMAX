Ensure Quality Cleaning

Make sure your cleaners leave everything spic and span!

Your clients will only be satisfied if the cleaner has done a good job and as a manager, it is your responsibility to ensure that! But cleaning inspections can get daunting. Right? Well, not anymore! Ensure quality cleaning with easy-breezy cleaning inspections through our system and keep your clients satisfied!

Rate the Cleaners

An easy system to rate the performance of your cleaners.

The profiles of your cleaners must speak for them. With our Inspection feature, you can also rate the cleaners for the job done by them. The ratings are recorded within the system, helping the cleaners maintain a reputation. If a cleaner has a good rating, the clients will pick them for the next job. It also keeps the cleaners motivated to work better!

Record Comments and Photos

Create detailed inspection reports with additional elements!

Cleaning inspections have a lot to cover. Our Inspection feature allows you to add comments and photos of the designated area. So, the client can have a clear explanation of what work was done and how the place looks after the cleaning. You can also take a before picture and share it with the after picture for a better impression.

Easy to Share With The Client

Zip! Zap! Share the reports with the clients right away!

Once the inspection reports have been created, you can share them with the clients with just one touch. There are several media added within the system where the reports can be shared. You can also generate different formats of the reports like PDFs, docs, etc. So, you can share them with the client in a suitable format on a suitable channel.
