Paper to Digital Cleaning Log

Everything is turning digital and so are the paper log sheets!

Tired of the age-old log keeping methods? Bid adieu to paper log sheets and let your cleaners hop on the latest tech. Our digital cleaning log not only helps managers create an all-encompassing log sheet but also helps cleaners update it by just a tap. They can upload pictures, report problems, and get instant help in real-time.

Real-Time information

Tracking and reporting made easy with real-time features!

Keep track of your cleaners with the latest GPS tracking features and help them report in real-time with our cleaning services management system. The system helps you keep track of where each cleaner is deployed, what work is assigned to them, and whether they have reached the site or not. Get real-time updates about work being done and completed by the cleaners.

Problem Reporting

In case of problems or emergencies, help is just a tap away!

Cleaners facing problems doing a job? Send help instantly! Our management system helps the cleaners report problems and send notifications to the managers. So, help can be sent without wasting any time. The cleaners or janitors can attach photos or send videos to report a problem and ask for help.

Analyze Problem Data

Take a look at the problem data anytime, and save it for future reports!

Once problems are reported by the janitors and cleaners, it is saved in the history of the system. The data can be used anytime to send a report to the client or create monthly or weekly reports. The managers can access this data anytime they want to proceed with the situation further.
