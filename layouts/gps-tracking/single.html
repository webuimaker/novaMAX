GPS Trail

Monitor security officers’ movements in real time

Powerful GPS trail feature allows supervisors to seamlessly track the movements of a guard on duty. It sends automatic alerts when the security officer stress away from the designated route. The tech empowers clients to stay up to date on the security arrangements at their site. By creating location centric reports, it cuts down on the costs and raise the level of patrol efficiency. GPS trailing can also be used as proactive maintenance and save money on unintentional mishandlings.

Geofence

Create geographical fencing for better coordination

Lay down location boundaries without marking anything physically or using any objects or symbols. Geofencing covers an area of specific radius and is used to provide accurate surveillance. It sets up triggers whenever a guard leaves the designated area, adding accountability to their actions. Also creates a safety net for a guard by designating certain areas as off-limits. The creative tracking features of Novagems help you maximise your resources and increase your market credibility.

Offline Tracking

Works even when a device is not connected to Internet

Bad network or low Internet connectivity can no longer hamper your security business. Offline tours work smoothly and capture all the necessary information like QR codes scanning and GPS proofs. The details are uploaded true the cloud-based system as soon as connectivity restores. Gives you the power to guard even the remotest of the places without having to worry whether the job will be done perfectly or not. Increases the efficiency of your operations and makes it easy to monitor your officers.

Location backed Reports

Picks the location details automatically

When protecting a location or a property, a security office may not get time to fill all the details in the daily briefing. In addition to clocking guard’s time at location and creating a GPS trail for the patrolling party, the app also collects location data for reporting. It captures the site information in real time with accuracy and provides the basis for enhancing the effectiveness of your services. Can also be used to remotely keep tabs on your company vehicles and their workers’ performance.
