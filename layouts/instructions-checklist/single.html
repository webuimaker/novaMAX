Add Job Instructions

No more long briefing sessions! Explain everything in the most precise manner!

Don’t let the cleaners and janitors get confused about the work orders. Use our templates to generate instructions and checklists that can be shared with them with just one tap. Through step-by-step instructions, the job can clearly be explained for better understanding.

Create Checklists for Tasks

Let them just ‘Check’ and you’ll know it’s done!

If a job requires the cleaners to work on multiple tasks, creating checklists is the best way to achieve it all! Use our cleaner management system and create checklists within. Share them with the cleaners who can mark the tasks done!

Add Attachments like Photos

Work explained better! Give clear instructions with photos.

For any complicated tasks that need explaining, you can also attach photos to the instructions. Make it clear and simpler with visual aids and the cleaners can have a better understanding of the task.

Track Task Progress

No need to bug the cleaners every thirty minutes! Track work progress in real-time!

Is the work done? No need to ask the cleaners. Just keep track of their work through the checklists shared with them. Once a checklist is all checked, it is cue for the managers to go for the inspection. So, no need to keep visiting the site to track task progress.
