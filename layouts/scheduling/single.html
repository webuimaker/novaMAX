Shift Planning

Fast & reliable shift planning assistance

Effective workplaces are built on strong systems for employee management. In most organizations, staff works round the clock. Relying on manual processes or excel sheets to manage their shifts can easily turn into an administrative nightmare. The shift planning tool of Novagems gives you the control to easily manage these fluctuations in the work schedule. Administrators can balance schedules, create rules, auto-approve requests, and change number of people working in a shift automatically to save time and add flexibility in operations. With shift planning feature, they can also allocate people to work on important projects and clear the backlogs in real time.

Open Shifts

Compatible technology to fill open shifts effortlessly

Once you have created a shift planning schedule, the feature enables you to fill open shifts effortlessly. It maximizes productivity by reducing the time spent of managing the workforce and improves the efficiency of individual departments. The feature automatically notifies staff members who meet the job criteria and reduces the overtime costs for the company. It also flags any scheduling actions that violate any pre-defined rules to give you the ease of maintaining the compliance. The employees can respond to open shifts through the app on their smartphones. In some cases, businesses also use this feature to allow their employees to sign up on open shifts or even swap their shifts to get a control over their working hours.

Shift Time & Cost Analysis

Digital analysis of time & cost, engineered for excellence

This cloud-based feature cuts the risks associated with overstaffed shifts, shorthanded Rush timings, and less-than-acceptable performance. It not only builds, fills, and manages best-fit schedules for your company, but also throws open a time and cost analysis to give you measurable financial metrics such as capital saved as a result of timely decisions. It considers various sets of options, identifies impacts and indicators, predicts the results over a period and helps you to consider if any changes are required in the current way of running your operations.

Shift Templates

Advanced templates that make scheduling easy

Useful when creating schedules, it brings an order to creating regular shifts. This feature gets the work done with efficiency and enables your staff to meet project deadlines. With the numerous shift templates already in the software, you can save hours in allocating work. The feature is easy to customize, and it also assists in reducing stress levels of an administrator to a minimum. You can now say goodbye to manual shift tracking and create schedules to improve accuracy.

Instant Employee Replacement

Committed to find immediate replacement for missing employees

Easy to use interface makes it easy to reschedule employees and adhere to efficient time management at workplace. The feature gives you powerful task management tools that make staff swapping, workflow analyzing, and schedule sharing easy. The automation of work scheduling maximizes productivity and manages the costs by optimizing the available staff.
